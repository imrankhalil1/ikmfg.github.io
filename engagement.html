How We Deliver

A clear, structured path from discovery to transformation to long‑term control.

Manufacturers don’t need another system overhaul or an open‑ended consulting engagement. They need a disciplined, practical approach that reveals the truth, fixes the root causes, and keeps the business running on facts. Our engagement model provides exactly that.

1. Diagnostic — Find the Truth

We begin by analyzing the last 12 months of production, comparing actual results to standards across material, labor, and factory overhead. Managerial and indirect costs are allocated based on real output to show the true cost of each product, customer, and line.

The result is a clear, data‑driven view of where margins are leaking — and why.

2. Implementation — Fix the Root Causes

Once the gaps are visible, we move into structured execution. This includes strengthening processes, improving data capture, tightening reporting workflows, and aligning costing elements with operational reality. BOMs, routings, and production assumptions are updated based on what is actually happening on the floor.

The goal is simple: eliminate guesswork and build a foundation of accurate, reliable operational data.

3. Control Tower — Maintain Accuracy & Visibility

Costing and operational truth require ongoing discipline. We provide continuous support to keep your data clean, your processes consistent, and your numbers reliable. This includes monthly costing reviews, variance analysis, BOM/routing maintenance, inventory reliability checks, and continuous improvement insights.

You gain a senior‑level partner ensuring your operations stay aligned, accurate, and financially sound.

A Practical, Low‑Risk, High‑Impact Approach

This model gives manufacturers clarity at every step — what we’re doing, why it matters, and how it improves margin, cash flow, and confidence in the numbers. It’s structured, transparent, and built for teams that want results without complexity.
