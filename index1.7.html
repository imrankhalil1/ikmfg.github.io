Mission

Helping manufacturers gain clarity over their numbers and build financial systems that drive profitable, sustainable growth.

Why This Mission Matters

Manufacturers operate in environments where small inaccuracies compound into major financial consequences. When costing, inventory, or systems fall out of sync with operational reality, leaders lose visibility into margin, cash flow, and performance.

The Challenges

  • Costing that doesn’t reflect how products are actually made
  • Inventory discrepancies that erode trust in the numbers
  • Systems that are under‑utilized or overly manual
  • Decisions made without reliable financial insight

How Our Mission Solves These Challenges

We focus on the root causes — not symptoms. By aligning financial data with operational truth, we help manufacturers make confident decisions, improve profitability, and build systems that scale with growth.

Founder

IK Manufacturing Finance is led by its Founder and CEO, Imran Khalil, a Dallas-based finance and operations business partner with more than 25 years of experience in manufacturing operations and financial leadership. He has held senior roles with global and mid-sized manufacturers including Newell Rubbermaid, Tetra Pak, Elkay Manufacturing, and Apogee Foods, partnering with executive teams and plant leadership to improve financial reporting, optimize inventory management, implement costing systems, and align financial performance with operational goals.

Imran’s expertise spans product costing, inventory optimization, financial planning and analysis, ERP systems, capital investment evaluation, and operational performance measurement. He has led major financial initiatives including capital investment projects exceeding $37 million, global financial planning processes, and multi-plant performance improvement programs across operations in the United States, Mexico, and China. Through IK Manufacturing Finance, he now brings this enterprise-level experience to small and mid-sized manufacturers that need experienced financial guidance but may not have the resources for a full-time CFO or finance team.

Philosophy

Our work sits at the intersection of finance, operations, and systems. Financial clarity only exists when the numbers match what’s happening on the shop floor. Our approach is hands‑on, practical, and grounded in operational truth.

Services

Three tightly connected areas that directly impact margin, cash flow, and confidence in your numbers.

  • Review bills of materials for completeness and accuracy

Inventory Reliability

  • Identify patterns in write‑offs and adjustments

  • Connect inventory reliability to cash flow and service levels

Systems & Automation

  • Design AI‑assisted checks for data quality
  • Ensure solutions fit team size and capacity
